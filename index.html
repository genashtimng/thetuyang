SHELL

Miri New Office Artwork Curation & Delivery

Concept:

People/Community
Balance/Harmony

The bedrock of Borneo is its indigenous people and their connection and knowledge of the land as keepers, and also as innovators.

The artistic elements by the various artists from different communities (indigenous and non-indigenous alike), and from Miri and wider Sarawak and Malaysia which carries the message of harmony - balancing human, their culture the natural environment - as well as the innovation (contemporary), hopes to evoke thought about how we can all always do better to achieve balance. Our curation carries the reminder that all of us co-exist and ideally, in balance.

That nature and innovation can co-exist. That tradition and modern day life can co-exist.

Summery

nspired by nature’s ecology, we are establishing the connection between People/Community (in this case, Shell employees) and the natural world around them in order to achieve Balance/Harmony.

By interpreting the art from the ground floor as the genesis from the earth/root/soil, to the top floors as the sun/sky/mountain top - it is telling a story of the layers of our ecology.

Growth from the soil to the tallest trees or the air on the mountain top (and vice versa - where rain from the clouds fall back to the earth) is just as nature intended.

This is the same way how Borneo indigenous communities gain inspiration in everything we do and create for hundreds of years.
